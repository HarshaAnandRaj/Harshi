My Dearest Love

Hello, My love.

This is something equivalent of my digital love letter to you. Even though, We only entered relationship few months back. The amount of affection and the bond we build was unheard of for me.

I am recounting our lives before we came into each others and we painted all red and white.

All the bad things we had to endure.. Brought us here.. To this one digital moment that just undo everything before and build us anew.

It is and always will be such a miracle to find you among all the people that are alive in this moment and I, For one, Seem to only come alive in your presence, My sweetheart.

Greatest is the man who has a woman on his shoulder.. It is not that of a burden. But, A privilege.

A privilege to love and be loved.

To endure and be endured.

To be held up and hold you up.

To hold your hand when shit hits the fan.

You’re not a burden, My love. You’re a privilege my eyes ever have a blessing to be graced with your beauty and smile.

I do not know where this goes and how we will be in the end.

But, I do know this.

I want you in reach of my hands..

Touch your lips…

And close enough to love you for lifetimes.

Yours - Hershey ♡
You are my miracle.
Always.
